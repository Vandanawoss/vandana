---
title: "How Many Ways Can We Define Online Learning? A Systematic Literature Review of Definitions of Online Learning (1988-2018)"
authors: "Singh, V., Thurman, A."
journal: "American Journal of Distance Education."
year: 2019
---

We conducted a systematic literature review over the last 30 years (1988 to 2018) to investigate the number and content of definitions of online learning. We collected 46 definitions from 37 resources and conducted a content analysis on these sets of definitions.
